## Obtain a valid license

To first get started, you need to obtain a **Zyrix Key** to utilize Zyrix Administartion.
Please visit [Obtaining License Key](/index) to learn how to obtain a Zyrix Key.


## Redeem your key
After obtaining your **Zyrix Key**, you need to create and register your server.
Head over to the [panel](https://my.zyrixadmin.com/) and press "Add Server".

1. Type in the name of your server in the first input box.
2. Paste your **Zyrix Key** into the second input box.
3. Once license is validated you can read [Configuration Guide](/config/getting-started) to start
setting up your Zyrix Admin.
